Maultaschen

How to make

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 eggs + 1 egg for egg wash
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Bacon
  • Ground burger
  • Nutmeg
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Spinach

    • preheat the saucepan and add water from a boiled kettle with a hint of salt Boil some maultaschen in a saucepan over a medium heat for five minutes. rince the maultaschen into a colander to get rid of the water. Serve in a bowl with cheese for extra flavour
