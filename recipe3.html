Bratwurst

How to make

  • 1 bratwurst sausage
  • preheat frying pan to a high heat with 1 tsp of olive oil
  • season sausage to taste using salt and pepper or other spices and herbs
  • place sausage on frying pan
  • fry for 10 minutes turning occasionally
  • place sausage on plate after sausage has browned
  • serve with any side dish of your choice
